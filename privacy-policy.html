Privacy Policy for My CoffeeCalc

Last updated: 07/18/2025

Overview

This privacy policy explains how My CoffeeCalc ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you.

Our app operates entirely on your device and does not transmit, store, or collect any personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Device information
  • Any other personal information

Apple App Store Analytics

While our app does not collect any data, Apple may automatically collect certain information through the App Store platform, including:

  • App download and installation data
  • App Store page views and interactions
  • Basic app performance metrics
  • Crash reports (if you have opted in to share analytics with Apple)

This information is collected by Apple, not by us, and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. You can control your analytics sharing preferences in your device's Settings app under Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.

Data Storage and Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information for us to store or secure. All app functionality operates locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no additional privacy considerations for children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no personal data for you to access, modify, or delete. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us using the information below.
